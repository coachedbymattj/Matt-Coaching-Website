FAQ

Questions, answered

Can't find what you're looking for? Ask us directly.

How much does treatment cost?

Every smile is different, so cost depends on your treatment and timeline. Your free consultation includes a clear, written quote and flexible 0% interest plans — with no obligation.

How long will I wear braces or aligners?

Most treatments take 9–24 months. Adults using clear aligners often finish in around 12 months. We'll give you a realistic estimate at your first visit.

Invisalign or braces — which is right for me?

Both work brilliantly. Aligners are removable and discreet; braces handle complex cases efficiently. Dr. Marsh will recommend the best fit for your goals and lifestyle.

What age should my child first be seen?

The American Association of Orthodontists recommends a first check-up by age 7. Early visits are about monitoring growth — most children won't need treatment yet.

Do you offer payment plans and take insurance?

Yes. We offer 0% interest monthly plans, accept most major insurance, and are FSA/HSA friendly. Our team will help you maximise every benefit available.
