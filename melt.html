What MELT is

A program for people who do not need motivation. They need structure.

MELT is for the trainer who has plateaued, the lifter who has lost the thread, the person who has done the work but cannot crack the next level alone.

It is not a beginner program. It is not a transformation. It is the same method I use 1:1, delivered in a cohort that holds you accountable to the long arc.

The four pillars

What the program is built on.

Pillar 01

Strength.

Foundational lifts, intelligently progressed. The non-negotiable base.

Pillar 02

Capacity.

Conditioning that supports the strength work rather than fights it.

Pillar 03

Recovery.

The work between the work. Sleep, nutrition, decompression.

Pillar 04

Habit.

The architecture that makes the rest of it survive a real life.

The 12 weeks

How the cohort runs.

Weeks 1 to 4

Foundations.

Reset position, dial in execution, build the base. Less load, more attention.

Weeks 5 to 8

Build.

Progressive overload, capacity work, habit consolidation. The middle block does most of the heavy lifting.

Weeks 9 to 12

Express.

Peak the work, measure the change, hand you a method you can take with you.
