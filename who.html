Who I am

Built through years of getting it wrong.

The short version of a long story. Where the work comes from, and why it looks the way it does.

The origin

I started as the client.

Like a lot of coaches, I came to this through my own body first. I trained hard for years without much to show for it. I chased programs. I copied people. I burned out, came back, burned out again.

The turning point was not a single moment. It was a slow shift in what I was paying attention to. Less to the noise, more to the signal. Less to the next thing, more to the boring thing that actually worked.

The work that compounds is rarely the work that gets sold.
What changed

I stopped chasing the workout. I started building the system.

Once I had a system that held, I started getting other people through the same door. Faster. Without the wasted years.

That is what coaching is, in my hands. Less programming, more attention. Less hype, more architecture.
